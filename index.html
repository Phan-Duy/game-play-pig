Let's Play Pig!

Rules
  • The first player to reach 100 points wins.
  • Players take turns rolling the die.
  • A turn ends when a player rolls a 1 or chooses to hold.
  • If a player rolls a 1, they lose all points earned during that turn.
  • If a player holds, the points earned during the turn are added to their total.
